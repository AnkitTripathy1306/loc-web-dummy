Round Details

  • Round 1: Quiz Round - 30 Minutes (8:30 AM to 9:00 AM)
    • > This round will be attempted by all the team mates individually.
    • > The average of the team score will be considered for the final sum.
    • > This round will be proctored online, so having a camera to your computer is mandatory and any window shift will result in disqualification of the member.
    • > Topics for this round will vary from general aptitude to basic coding knowledge.
  • Round 2: Programming Round - 2 Hours (10:00 AM to 12:00 PM)
    • > This round will be attempted by the team unitedly.
    • > This round will consist of 2 sections. Section A will have 3 or 4 questions and    Section B will have 1 question. Time Limit: Section A (2hrs), Section B (1 hr)
    • > Team members can discuss with one another online or sit together during this  round.
    • > Strict plagiarism check will be enabled and any misconduct will lead to disqualification of the whole team.
